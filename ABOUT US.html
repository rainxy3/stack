ABOUT STACK KNOWLEDGE

The Stack Knowledge is a website that will provide students with a multitude of options that can aid their study. particularly, in the four core courses, such as Mathematics, science, English, and Filipino are the subjects covered. This website will feature several classes to add to their Learnings and quizzes to assess students' ability. Students can now improve their grades with these website by using the capability and knowledge in the four major disciplines simply by using the website Stack Knowledge.

STACK KNOWLEDGE GOAL

The main goal of this essay is to propose the creation of a website called Stack Knowledge, which would be a tool for junior high students to learn and enhance their skills in the subjects of Mathematics, Science, English and Filipino. The website would include a short quiz or assessment at the end of each lesson to test students' proficiency, as well as local school recommendations for upcoming senior high school students. The goal is to help students cope with their studies and be more prepared for future lessons and discussions.
The website will provide comfort and encouragement for students to
become wiser and smarter.
