Biodiversity and Evolution

a. The maximum population size an environment can support

b. pertains to the number of organisms of the same species living in a certain place.

c. It refers to the variety of life in an area.

d. A major problem in lakes, rivers and ponds is eutrophication, one of the effects of water pollution


2. These are the example of limiting factors, Except:

a. The availability of food

b. Water

c. Money

d. Living conditions


3. What do you call the happening when last member of species die.

a. Pre-colossal

b. Jurassic

c. Extinction

d. Endangered Species


4. A major problem in lakes, rivers and ponds is eutrophication, one of the effects of ____?

a. Air Pollution

b. Land Pollution

c. Soil Pollution

d. Water Pollution

a. Deforestation, agricultural activities, and mining activities

b. Cleaning the houses

c. Coastal areas conversion to beach resorts, residential areas

d. Dynamite fishing and merogamy
