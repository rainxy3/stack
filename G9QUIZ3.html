MATHEMATICS | GRADE 9

Quadrilaterals

1. If a quadrilateral has four equal sides, then it is a square.

a. True

b. False

c. Maybe

d. None of the above


2. A quadrilateral is a closed shape and a type of polygon that has six sides, three vertices and three angles.

a. True

b. False

c. Maybe

d. None of the above


3. A trapezoid is a quadrilateral with exactly one pair of ____ sides.

a. Parallel

b. Congruent

c. Perpendicular

d. Equal


4. It is a quadrilateral with two sets of parallel sides.

a. Trapezoid

b. Square

c. Kite

d. Parallelogram


5. Which of the following is TRUE about quadrilateral

a. Every quadrilateral has six sides, three vertices and three angles.

b. The total of its interior angles is 360 degrees

c. The total of its exterior angles is 360 degrees

d. None of the above


6. What do a rhombus and a rectangle have in common?

a. They are both parallelograms

b. They always have four right angles

c. They are not polygons

d. They have five sides


7. A square can be a rhombus

a. True

b. False

c. Maybe

d. None of the above


8. Which of the following properties is TRUE about kites?

a. Consecutive angles are supplementary

b. Opposite sides are parallel

c. Exactly one pair of opposite angles are congruent

d. None of the above


9. Which of the following properties is true about parallelograms?

a. Opposite sides are congruent

b. Diagonals are perpendicular

c. Diagonals are congruent

d. None of the above


10. The sum of two adjacent angles of a parallelogram is equal to ____.

a. 90 degrees

b. 180 degrees

c. 360 degrees

d. None of the above
