MATHEMATICS | GRADE 7

Polynomials

1. What is the leading coefficient of the polynomial 2x+5

a. 2x

b. 2

c. 5

d. None of the above


2. It is a polynomial with three terms

a. Monomial

b. Binomial

c. Trinomial

d. Polynomial


3. A polynomial with a degree of five

a. Quadratic

b. Quartic

c. Quintic

d. None of the above


4. What is the degree of the polynomial 10x+5

a. 0

b. 1

c. 2

d. None of the above


5. It is a kind of algebraic expression where each term is a constant, a variable or a product of a constant and variable in which the variable has a whole number (non-negative number) exponent.

a. Quadratic

b. Standard Form

c. Polynomial

d. Binomial


6. In a polynomial expression 3x+3y+2, 3x is called a ____.

a. Coefficient

b. Variable

c. Term

d. None of the above


7. It is polynomial of degree zero.

a. Linear

b. Constant

c. Degree

d. None of the above


8. If the polynomial is in standard form, the first term is called the ____.

a. Leading Variable

b. Leading Term

c. Leading Coefficient

d. None of the above


9. It is defined as a fixed number that is multiplied to a variable. It is a constant or a number.

a. Constant

b. Literal Coefficient

c. Numerical Coefficient

d. None of the above


10. If the terms of a polynomial are arranged from the term with the highest degree, up to the term with the lowest degree, it is in a____.

a. Standard Form

b. Quadratic Form

c. Linear Form

d. None of the above
